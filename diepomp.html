"diepomp", whose real name is Stefan Lubbe, is a South African musician and producer known for his eclectic mix of genres, including elements of rock, pop, electronic, and Afrikaans folk music. He grew up in South Africa and started his music career at a young age. Lubbe's music is known for its raw and honest lyrics, which often touch on themes of love, heartbreak, and personal growth. He has a unique and dynamic sound that has gained him a loyal following among South African audiences.

"diepomp" has released several albums, including "Die Rysmaker" and "Die Oomblik is Nou". These albums have been well-received by critics and fans alike and have cemented Lubbe's status as a talented and innovative musician in the South African music industry. His music has been described as a refreshing and unique take on Afrikaans music, and he has been recognized for his talent with several awards and nominations.

In addition to his work as a musician, "diepomp" is also a respected music producer and has collaborated with several notable South African artists. He has collaborated with popular artists such as Anton Goosen, Adam Tas, Jakkie Louw and Fatman among others. Lubbe's production work is known for its quality and innovation, and he has earned a reputation as a very talented music creator, singer and producer in South Africa.
