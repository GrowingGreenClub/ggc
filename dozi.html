Dozi is a popular Afrikaans singer and songwriter from South Africa. He has been active in the music industry since the 1980s and has released numerous albums over the years.

Some of Dozi's most well-known songs include "Wind en Weer", "Jan Asseblief Tog Squeeze My", "Houtkruis", and "Lyn".

In addition to his music career, Dozi has also acted in a number of South African films and TV shows. He is known for his down-to-earth personality and is popular among his fans for his engaging live performances.

Dozi has won several awards for his music, including a SAMA (South African Music Award) for Best Afrikaans Traditional Music Album in 2014. He continues to tour and perform across South Africa and is highly respected in the Afrikaans music industry.
