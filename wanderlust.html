Wanderlust is a South African musical group based in Klerksdorp, known for their unique blend of rock music. Their sound has won over many fans both locally and internationally. The group consists of four members, including lead singer Heinrich (Ollie) Ollewagen, guitarist Adriaan Joubert, bassist and backup vocalist Janus Byleveldt, and drummer Andre Pretorius.

Wanderlust's music is characterized by its soulful vocals, catchy melodies, and upbeat rhythms, and is influenced by a range of musical genres. They perform both covers and original songs in English and Afrikaans. Some of their most popular songs include "WORLD," "STELENBOSCH," and "Calling," with their latest release "DANS OP DIE HORISON" already gaining traction.

In addition to their music, Wanderlust is known for their energetic and engaging live performances. They have performed at numerous festivals and events throughout South Africa, building a dedicated following of fans who eagerly anticipate their next performance. Overall, Wanderlust is a captivating musical experience that resonates with audiences, evoking a sense of unity and passion in their fans.
